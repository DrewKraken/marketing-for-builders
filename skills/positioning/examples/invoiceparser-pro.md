# Example: InvoiceParser Pro

A worked before → after from the `positioning` skill, run on a real product. For the full multi-case evaluation, see [`evals/RESULTS.md`](../../../evals/RESULTS.md).

## Input (how the product was first described)

> "AI-powered accounts payable automation. Extract, validate, review, and export invoice data for bookkeepers, accounting firms, and small businesses." (followed by a long feature list — extraction engine, currency resolver, ERP integrations, etc.)

## Without the skill (baseline)

> **Tagline:** "Every invoice, extracted and posted in seconds."

Led with the mechanism ("AI-powered accounts payable automation platform"), piled the features into the value prop, and addressed bookkeepers, firms, and small businesses all at once.

## With the skill

> **Positioning:** For bookkeepers and accounting firms processing dozens of invoices a week who spend most of their time keying data from PDFs into accounting software, InvoiceParser Pro is an AP automation tool that reads, validates, and posts every invoice directly to QuickBooks Online, Xero, or Zoho Books — unlike manual entry or basic OCR that still leaves the cleanup to the bookkeeper.
>
> **One-liner:** Bookkeepers: cuts invoice entry from minutes to zero — InvoiceParser Pro posts it for you.
>
> **Value proposition:** For bookkeepers and accounting firms processing dozens of invoices a week, manual data entry *is* the job — not a side task. InvoiceParser Pro reads each invoice in under 5 seconds and posts it directly to QuickBooks Online, Xero, or Zoho Books, so you skip the retyping. It extracts every field — header, line items, multi-currency amounts, bank routing — and each correction trains the next extraction from that vendor, so accuracy improves with use. Routine invoices move untouched; you only review the ones that need a decision.

## What changed

- **Led with the outcome** (invoices posted for you), not the mechanism ("AI-powered automation").
- **Narrowed the audience** from three segments to the one that feels the pain most acutely.
- **Positioned against the real alternative** — manual entry and basic OCR — not a named competitor.

The linter passed both the one-liner and the value proposition clean after one revision pass.
